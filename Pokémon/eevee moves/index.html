Eevee moves

Eevee has an extremely varied move pool, thanks in part to both it's normal typing and status as the "evolution Pokémon", however, some notable options include:

Swift: a somewhat powerful move, with 60 base attack power and 20 PP, this move in particular never misses, making it particularly useful in high level battles, especially against flying types.

Quick Attack: a classic attack for most normal types, Eevee can learn quick attack fairly early on, at level 10, however, this does mean that the attack's attack power is low, to compensate, it nearly always goes first, regardless of the Pokémon's speed stat.

Baby-doll Eyes: another early move, learned through level up at level 15, this attack is plenty useful early on, lowering the attack of the opponent, not by much, and there are plenty alternatives, however, early on, it does have it's uses.

Bite: a classic dark type move, it has decent attack power and good accuracy, however, it is particularly useful as it allows eevee to deal x2 effective attacks to ghost types, which are normally immune to most of eevee's move pool
