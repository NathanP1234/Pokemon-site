Bulbasaur moves

Bulbasaur has a much more varied move pool than most of the other Pok√©mon on this website thanks to its dual typing and high base defense stat

Sludge Bomb: a good poison type attack with 90 Ap and a max of 16 PP, sludge bomb can be used to make the normal sludge attack deal double damage if used the next turn, as well as the fact it has a 30% chance to poison the target, allowing great chip damage if it lands

Leech Seed: an amazing HP stealing move, it has really good accuracy, though only a base of 10 PP and a max of 16, it seeds the target, dealing chip damage as well as healing half the damage done back to itself, combining this with a big root, and you can heal good amounts of HP

sleep powder: a grass type attack with fairly low accuracy (75%), this attack puts the opponent to sleep, allowing you to attack it without having to worry about getting hit yourself, though the sleep status effect wears off rather quickly

Solar Beam: a grass type attack that takes a turn to charge up, as well as causing sunny weather, which boosts fire attacks, this attack has massive drawbacks, however, it has 120 AP and 100% accuracy, meaning that, unless the opponent has a boosted evasion stat, this attack rarely misses
